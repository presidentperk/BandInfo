Red Hot Chili Peppers

Anthony Kiedis, Flea, Chad Smith, Josh Klinghoffer

The Red Hot Chili Peppers are an American rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1983. Currently, the band consists of the aforementioned members, but a number of others have been a part since its formation in 1983. They are one of the best-selling bands ever, having sold more than 80 million records worldwide. They have been nominated for 16 Grammy Awards and have won 6.
